About Us

Palisar was founded in 1988 by Mickeal Linigan. Mickel was a outdoor enthusiast with a passion in snowboarding. With a group of friends who ended up as business partners he started out the company selling snowboards and bindings. Over the years the company grew in size and now we have exapnded out product line to a larger section of winter equipment and apparel. Palisar promises to maintain the high standards of quality service, top of the line products, and deliver an excited experience as you take your new snowboard or skis down the slopes.
